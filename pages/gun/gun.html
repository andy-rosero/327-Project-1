You raise your rifle before your ghost even finishes calling out to you, pulling the trigger for a split second as several shots ring out of the automatic weapon. The dreg crumples to the ground, dead in front of you.

“Nice shot guardian!” your ghost says as he materializes beside you, “I definitely chose the right person for the job. Now let's go, the ship’s signature isn’t that far ahead.”

The two of you continue into the adjoining room, this one much bigger than the last. Suspended at the center of the room is what looks like a ship, hanging on only by wires.

“Last City here we come” the Ghost chirps, “Let me just dust off my hot-wiring skills.”

The ghost dematerializes into the ship. Within a minute, the ship becomes the second thing to come alive today. Centuries-old technology begins whirring and beeping, a loud groan of the engine kicking back momentarily as it hums to life.

You feel a tug in your core as your ghost teleports you into the cockpit.

“Driving lessons later. I can get us there safely. Just sit back and enjoy the ride.”
